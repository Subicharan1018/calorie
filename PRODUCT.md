# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Urban Tamil adults, aged 25–45, who are health-aware and use their smartphones daily. Context: They want to track their daily calories accurately using local South Indian food, needing an experience that feels culturally native rather than an adapted Western app.

## Product Purpose
"Kalori" is an Android-first Flutter calorie deficit tracker tailored for the South Indian diet. It serves as a pocket nutritionist that allows users to input the vegetables they have, get South Indian recipe suggestions (with AI generation), and log their meals with precise ICMR-NIN nutrition data. Success looks like users easily maintaining their deficit without having to guess the macros of their local cuisine.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Culturally Authentic, Personal, and Focused. It should feel like a high-end personal nutritionist. No generic "health app" tropes.

## Anti-references
- "Health app blue" defaults.
- Generic sad faces or placeholders for empty states (use culturally specific illustrations like an empty brass thali or kolam grid).
- Western taxonomy for food categories (e.g. use Leafy Green, Root, Gourd instead of generic categories).
- Food icon clichés in logos (typography-only wordmark).

## Design Principles
1. **Culturally Resonant, Not Stereotypical:** Embed Tamil language alongside English organically, and use South Indian culinary terms correctly.
2. **Premium Precision:** Typography, spacing, and layout should feel high-end, uncluttered, and precise. Pixel-perfect spacing and shadow depth.
3. **Action-Oriented Empathy:** Empty states and onboarding should guide the user warmly and intelligently.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Touch Targets:** Minimum 48x48dp.
- **Semantics:** All icons must have Semantics labels in both English and Tamil.
- **Contrast:** AA minimum contrast ratio.
- **Scaling:** Layouts must support and be tested at 1.0× and 1.3× dynamic font scaling.
- **Color Independence:** Color is never the only indicator (use shapes/icons in charts/UI).
